Dedan Kimathi University of Technology

Dedan Kimathi University of Technology is strategically position in the central region of Kenya, 160 km North of the capital Nairobi and 7 km from Nyeri town.The institution was started in the year 1972 as an institute of Technology by the local community. The university coined its name from a fallen, well-known, outspoken MAU MAU hero and leader Dedan Kimathi who led the people of central to resist against the colonial masters. It was not until 2007 when the institution was made to be a constituent college of Jomo Kenyatta University of Technology. In the year 2012 it was granted full charter and it was renamed Dedan Kimathi University of Technology from its former name Kimathi University college of Technology
