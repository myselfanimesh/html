Welcome to Kitaima primary school

Kitaima primary school is one of the public primary schools in Bomet county. It was started in the year 1987 after splitting from Kiplokyi primary school. The school is strategically position at the junction of the three villages which are: Kitaima, Chemoiben and lalakin villages. The school has attracted children from the three villages and even beyond extending to Kamobiriri,Kiplokyi and Kapsigowo villages. Since it was started it has commendably produced many resource people in this country ranging from lawyers, computer scientists,Engineers, Accountants, Nurses, Teachers and many more proffessionals. Currently the school has a population of 450 pupils from Baby class all the way to class 8. The shool motto is"Discipline and devotion for success"
